Gym Journal

Log your workouts with minimal clutter

Get Started

An app designed with ease of use and minimalism in mind. Like a virtual notebook, log your sets and reps during your workout without any distractions. Edit your workouts on your computer and log them on your phone, this app is designed to be used with multiple devices, making it all the easier to edit workouts!
Share workouts with friends and compare your benchmarks with other users, Gym Journal can help you take your training to the next level.

Pricing

To keep this app as accessible for everyone, we have various tiers to cater to different types of people and their preferences:

Free Tier $0/mo
Create and edit routines. Log workouts and view your workout history. The barebones functionality of this app, enabling you to keep track of your workouts.
Basics Tier $5/mo
Everything from Free Tier and basic historical graphs (volume, load, reps). Rest times and timed exercises now get a timer.
Advanced Tier $10/mo
Everything from Basics Tier and graphs get an upgrade. Graphs for individual exercises are shown (1 rep max, max reps). Benchmark against other active users. Share routines with other users.
